Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Easy Pro Digital (“we”, “us”, or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and online services (collectively, the “Site”). By using the Site, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

1) What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, or region), and provide analytics so we can improve performance.

2) How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to: (a) enable core functionalities of the Site; (b) analyze traffic and performance; (c) personalize content; and (d) support marketing and advertising activities in compliance with applicable laws.

3) Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary (Essential) – Required for the Site to function (e.g., security, load balancing, session management). These cannot be switched off.
  • Performance & Analytics – Help us understand how visitors use the Site (e.g., pages visited, time on page) so we can improve usability and content.
  • Functional – Remember user choices (e.g., language, region) to provide enhanced, more personalized features.
  • Marketing & Advertising – Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness. May be set by us or by third-party partners.

4) Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties providing services on our Site (for example, analytics providers or advertising networks). These third parties may collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites, subject to their own policies.

5) Cookie Duration

Cookies may be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored until they expire or you delete them). Typical durations range from a few minutes to up to 24 months, depending on the cookie’s purpose.

Category Purpose Typical Duration
Essential Security, session, load balancing Session to 12 months
Analytics Usage statistics, performance insights 1–24 months
Functional Preferences (language, region) 1–12 months
Marketing Personalized ads, conversion tracking 1–24 months

6) Managing Your Preferences

You can control cookies through your browser settings and, where available, by using the preferences below. Note that blocking some types of cookies may impact your experience on the Site.

You can also clear or block cookies in your browser settings.

7) Do Not Track & Regional Rights

Our Site may not respond to “Do Not Track” signals due to the lack of industry standards. If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the CCPA. If you are located in the EU/EEA, you may have rights under the GDPR. Please see our Privacy Policy for more information and how to exercise your rights.

8) Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Effective Date”. Continued use of the Site after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9) Contact Us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or your cookie preferences, please contact us at support@easyprodigital.com.
